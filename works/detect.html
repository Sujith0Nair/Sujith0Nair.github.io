002: XR Developer at Detect Technologies

By converging IoT's smart sensing with XR visualization, we're enabling real-time defect detection and diagnosis, dramatically accelerating factory troubleshooting and minimizing downtime.

"Transcending physical limitations, XR empowers us to visualize the unseen, interact with the impossible, and ultimately engineer a safer, more intuitive world where human potential knows no bounds."

Visit Detect Technologies Website

Key Responsibilities & Achievements

  • Design and develop the core 3D visual positioning system for AR/VR/MR platforms.
  • Create intuitive and real-time visualizations of equipment, personnel, and potential hazards.
  • Optimize the visual experience for clarity, performance, and ease of operator comprehension.
  • Integrate real-world spatial data from sensors to provide accurate and contextual awareness within XR.
  • Develop visual cues and alerts for proximity warnings and safe operating zone boundaries.
  • Design and implement user interface elements within the XR environment for system interaction.
  • Ensure robust and low-latency integration of dynamic sensor data into the 3D visualization.
  • Collaborate with UX and safety teams to refine the visual design based on user needs and safety requirements.
