003 : Project Trainee at ISRO

Pioneering real-time 3D programming for immersive satellite visualization and dynamic simulation, enabling enhanced understanding and interaction with complex orbital mechanics and Earth observation data.

"To chart the cosmos is to chart ourselves; each celestial discovery illuminates not just the vastness above, but the boundless potential within."

Key Responsibilities & Achievements

  • Research and implement accurate physics models relevant to satellite motion (e.g., gravity, atmospheric drag, solar radiation pressure).
  • Develop algorithms for precise orbit propagation based on selected satellite parameters and time.
  • Implement coordinate transformations between different reference frames (e.g., Earth-centered inertial, Earth-centered Earth-fixed, local tangent plane).
  • Design and implement the core logic for determining the satellite's position and orientation at a given time.
  • Validate the accuracy of the physics simulations against real-world data or established models.
  • Optimize physics calculations for performance within the simulation environment.
