001: Oculus VR Developer at SV.CO

Crafting serene VR backyard garden escapes, bringing the tranquility of nature to your couch for effortless relaxation and mindful moments.

"Blurring the lines between reality and the digitally crafted, VR's immersive power isn't just about escapism, it's about forging new realities that elevate our perspectives and fundamentally reshape how we live, learn, and connect in the physical world."

YouTube

Key Responsibilities & Achievements

  • 3D Environment Design: Create and optimize the 3D backyard environment, including landscaping, foliage, and architectural elements.
  • Interaction and Navigation Development: Implement intuitive VR interactions for users to explore and interact with the garden.
  • Audio Integration: Incorporate ambient sounds and spatial audio to enhance immersion and create a relaxing atmosphere.
  • Performance Optimization: Optimize the VR experience for smooth performance on the Oculus Go platform.
  • User Interface (UI) Design: Design and implement a user-friendly UI for navigation and settings within the VR environment.
  • Testing and Quality Assurance: Thoroughly test the VR experience to ensure stability, comfort, and a bug-free environment.
  • Platform Integration: Ensure seamless integration and deployment on the Oculus Go platform.
  • User Experience (UX) Design: Focus on creating a comfortable, intuitive, and enjoyable experience for users seeking relaxation and meditation.
